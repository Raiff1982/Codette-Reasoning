Core defense layers

AEGIS

Ethical governance layer. Intercepts and audits outputs before delivery, enforcing value alignment and adversarial input resistance.

Ethical governance

CloakSeal Protocol

Active threat containment. Triggered by confirmed hostile artifact detection; isolates and neutralizes without system disruption.

Threat containment

EchoLeak Manifest

Signal integrity monitoring. Documents and locks cross-system leakage events to prevent unauthorized data propagation.

Signal integrity

CognitionCocooner

Privacy-preserving persistent memory. Encapsulates sensitive context into sealed cocoons — retained but not exposed to external calls.

Memory privacy

SENTINAL Framework

Overarching AI safety scaffold. Governs inter-agent trust boundaries, enforces role separation, and monitors system-level drift.

Safety scaffold

Why this matters for economic research

Data integrity

Economic analysis is only as reliable as its inputs. AEGIS and EchoLeak monitoring directly address adversarial contamination of AI-assisted research pipelines.

Privacy preservation

CognitionCocooner aligns with OpenAI's stated requirement for privacy-preserving data use — sensitive researcher or participant data stays sealed in cocoons.

Reproducibility

SENTINAL's drift monitoring ensures the AI system behaves consistently across runs — a foundational requirement for peer-reviewable economic findings.

Bias auditing

AEGIS includes active bias detection within its governance layer, directly supporting the exchange's focus on unequal access and fair economic outcomes.
